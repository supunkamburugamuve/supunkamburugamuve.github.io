Hi I'm Supun Kamburugamuve, a PhD Candidate in Computer Science department at Indiana University Bloomington. My research advisor is Prof. Geoffrey Fox and my research interest includes large scale real time analysis of sensor data, distributed stream processing and control of mobile robots through cloud based processing. Prior to pursuing my PhD, I worked for three years in an open source middleware startup called WSO2 Inc as a project lead for the product WSO2 ESB.

Projects

  • WebPlotViz - Browser based 3D visualisation of time series data
  • IoTCloud - An open source framework for IoT and Sensor Centric Applications
  • CloudMooc - MOOC for cloud computing
  • WSO2 ESB - Open Source Enterprise Service Bus, project lead before leaving WSO2.
